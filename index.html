Americans Messenger Bot


OR Install it for Your Slack Team

Setting up Americans Bot for Slack is simple. Click Add to Slack to add it for your team to play with :)

Once added to Slack, simply type /americans slash command followed by location in any channel:

/americans population of Chicago, IL

Americans bot responds with requested location population data stats from census.gov

>~2,720,546 people live in Chicago city, IL
Population density: 11,944 p/mi²

About Americans Bot

USA census.gov provides a lot of interesting population data info. Why not have a bot to query it?

P.S.: next release of Americans bot will provide more info on USA housing, race, age, education, and incomes.
