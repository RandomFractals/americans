OR Install it for Your Slack Team

Setting up Americans Bot in Slack is simple. Just click the button to log in to your Slack account and choose a team!

Once installed for your Slack team, simply type /americans slash command followed by location (e.g. Chicago, IL). Americans bot responds with requested location population data stats from census.gov.

About Americans Bot

USA census.gov provides a lot of population data info, so why not have a bot to query it?
